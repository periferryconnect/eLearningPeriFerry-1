Reflect on Your Choices

Thank you for participating!

Did you find it difficult to make your choices?
For queer and transgender people, these decisions are not just an activity. They are the reality of everyday life.

Many privileges that are easy for some to take for granted are hard-fought or completely out of reach for LGBTQIA+ people.

The next time you find something is easy, remember: not everyone has the same choices, or gets to make them safely.

Let’s work together to build a world where no one has to choose between safety, love, identity, and acceptance.
